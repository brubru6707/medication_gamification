🌟 ANIMATION SYSTEM DETAILS
================================================================

IDLE ANIMATION TIMELINE:
  0.0-1.0 sec: Normal state (no animation)
  1.0-3.0 sec: Gradual build-up (0% → 100% intensity)
  3.0+ sec: Full idle animation

ANIMATION COMPONENTS:
  Squish .... ±8% scale change (breathing)
  Bob ....... ±5 pixels vertical (floating)
  Sway ...... ±3 pixels horizontal (rocking)
  Rotate .... ±2 degrees (tilting)

TRIGGERS:
  ✅ Character not moving
  ✅ Character on ground
  ✅ 1 second elapsed

STOPS WHEN:
  ❌ Any arrow key pressed
  ❌ Character starts moving
  ❌ Character jumps

================================================================
🎬 2.5D CAMERA SYSTEM
================================================================

DEPTH LAYERS:
  Layer 0 ... Ground (farthest, moves least)
  Layer 1 ... Mid platforms
  Layer 2 ... High platforms (closest, moves most)
  Layer 10 .. Characters (always on top, 2D)

PLATFORM SHADING (3D Effect):
  Top Surface ... Bright green (#00DD00)
  Front Face .... Medium green (#009900)
  Right Side .... Dark green (#006600)

ROTATION EFFECTS:
  X Offset ...... sin(angle) * depth * 30
  Y Offset ...... cos(angle) * depth * 10
  Scale ......... 1 - abs(sin(angle)) * 0.1
